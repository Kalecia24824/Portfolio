Short Stories at the Campfire

These are tales told by the campfire!

There once was a girl...

January 8th, 2025

There once was a girl who looked among the stars from afar. Every night she would take out her telescope at her spot to view the stars. And when she did, the girl was able to see a variety of colors. Pinks, blues and purples galore! However, time files as the girl must head back home. Now at home, the girl wishes she could play with the stars from afar.

The Golden Butterfly

March 22nd, 2025

In the south, there is a legend about a golden butterfly. So obscured that the butterfly can only be seen every once in a blue moon. In order to see the golden butterfly, the moon must be full and the night supper still. Only then, you will a shimmering golden light. Be quick lest you miss the golden butterfly who will grant you a wish.
